Motion Design

Midterm: 10 Principles of Good Design

This project aims to animate designer Dieter Rams’ Ten Principles of Good Design. In this short writing, Rams talks about what he believes to be the criteria of good design. By going through the design process , I was able to create an animation from scratch and understand how each step contribute to the final product.

Mood Board

In my mood board, I selected various products Dieter Rams designed, as well as designs from Apple to convey a calm, minimalistic feeling.

Design Board

My design boards have a persistent light grey background dark grey text. The overall style is monochromatic and calm. There are moments of scale shifts and color change to create visual interests.

Final: Fair Commercial

This project animates the commerial of the app “fair”. The app allows people to own cars in a new way. The original commercial is playful and light-mooded; I want to replicate that in my animation using colorful illustrations.

Mood Board

After researching similar commercials, I chose playful illustrations with bright colors and dynamic compositions, which best grab viewers' attention.

Design Board

For the design boards, I used the orange from the app logo with a complementary blue as my major colors. After I finished my design boards and started animating, I realized my design boards did not work for a couple of scenes because I did not think about the transitions in between, so I had to remake parts of the design boards in my animation.
