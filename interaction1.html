Interaction Design 1

Sleep Garden

The Sleep Garden is a mobile app that help people form healthy habits that are beneficial to their sleep. By gamifying the process of habit-forming, the app prompts users to repeat daily healthy habits. In the Sleep Garden, users will grow plants that represent their habits and eventually form a garden.

Personas

I conducted both quantitative and qualitative research. I researched the science facts on sleep. I also interviewed 10 people, gained insights from their responses, and created two personas for my app.

Patty's User Journey

The user journey demonstrates the desired outcomes of using the app. It would facilitate her to cut one coffee in the afternoon, do some simple exercises after she arrives home, and meditate before she sleeps. With a healthier lifestyle, she can attain higher-quality sleep.

Low Fidelity Wireframes

In my low-fidelity prototype, I focused on the onboarding process and the flow of the entire app. Because the Sleep Garden has a graphical interface, I put in rough drawings even in low-fidelity wireframes to give interviewees context.

High Fidelity Wireframes

Based on the critique and user testing results, I added more features related to sleep. It also has more refined illustrations and micro-interactions.
