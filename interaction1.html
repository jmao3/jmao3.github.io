Sleep Garden

The Sleep Garden is a mobile app that help people form healthy habits that are beneficial to their sleep. By gamifying the process of habit-forming, the app prompts users to repeat healthy habits on a day-today basis. In the Sleep Garden, users will grow plants that represent their habits and eventually form a garden.
