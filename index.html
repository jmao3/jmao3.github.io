Zagat Gourmet

Zagat Gourmet is an extension of the current Zagat website, which is a restaurant review website. The new extension, like Quora, provides user-generated contents by allowing users to ask and answer questions. In that way, users can get more insightful information that is hard to acquire otherwise.

User Flow

The current restaurant review sites either offer users an algorithmically generated list or reviews by professional reviewers. However, through my research, people prefer suggestions from their friends. By building a community of foodies, Zagat Gourmet allows more interactions between users.

Personas

I came up with two personas after researching potential users. Lucia is really interested in sharing her experience, while George wants more information and is less likely to write reviews.

Sitemap First Draft

On my first try at the sitemap, I over-complicated things. No one besides me could understand it. I failed to realize the point of the sitemap is for communication. However, this version of sitemap helped me sort out all the interactions.

Sitmap Revised

I revised my sitemap to make it more readable. Although it loses a lot of information, it has a clearer hiearchy.

Low Fidelity Wireframes

The low fidelity wireframes helped me sort through the basic interactions. Because I thought about most of them in my original sitemap, wireframing was not too hard.

High Fidelity Wireframes

In order make high fidelity wireframes, I tried to align my visual style with the original Zagat website. I used same fonts and color palettes, as well as certain UI elements.
